RULES:
This is a standard game of tic tac toe. 
First to three in a row wins!
If nobody can successfully get three in a row, its a Tie!

The board coordinates are as follows:

|--------------|
| 02 | 12 | 22 |
|--------------|
| 01 | 11 | 21 |
|--------------|
| 00 | 10 | 20 |
|--------------|

To select your move, 
simply type the coordinate in which you wish to place a piece.

Then hit Enter.
